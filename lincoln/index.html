Welcome to Lincoln Farms - Where Happy Cows and Friendly Horses Call Home

At Lincoln Farms, we believe that life is better with animals — and we’re proud to share our farm with visitors of all ages. Nestled in the beautiful countryside, our farm is home to gentle cows, playful calves, majestic horses, and a whole lot of fresh air and fun. Whether you’re here to learn about farm life, enjoy a guided tour, or simply meet our amazing animals, you’re always welcome.

What We Offer

  • Guided Farm Tours
  • Horseback Riding (for all skill levels)
  • Petting Areas for Kids
  • Seasonal Events & Farm Festivals
  • Fresh Dairy Products
  • Birthday Parties & Group Visits
